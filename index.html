About

I'm an ex-chef turned developer. At the age of 23, I decided to embark on a journey to find my passion in life and leave the restaurant industry behind. I wanted to do this so that I wouldn't be 40 years old, burnt out, and drained from working jobs I simply didn't love. After doing a massive deep dive into career opportunities, I found myself wanting to be part of the tech industry. It's booming and always changing the world, therefore I explored the option of development by enrolling in a basics of web design course. Upon completion, I was hooked. I started picking up every course about every language I stumbled upon. In my first week, I was creating sites from scratch. Over the next couple of months, I was building web apps and learning new languages. Once I built up my confidence, I defined my skillset by dedicating my education and practice to JavaScript, primarly focused on ReactJS. I am a self taught developer who is passionate, adaptable, and eager to break into the industry. I have made learning into my passion and developed a logical mind geared towards problem solving, as well as a penchant for being the very best at anything placed in front of me.
